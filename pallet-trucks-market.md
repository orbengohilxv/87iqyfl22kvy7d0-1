<p><p>The pallet truck market consists of two main types: manual and electric pallet trucks. Manual pallet trucks are operated by human effort, relying on a hydraulic pump to lift and move pallets, making them economical and suitable for smaller operations. In contrast, electric pallet trucks are powered by batteries, providing greater ease of use and efficiency, especially in larger warehouses or logistics environments. Both types enhance material handling efficiency, catering to different operational needs and preferences.</p></p>
