<p><p>The Pure Neem Oil market comprises three main types: seed extract oil, leaf extract oil, and bark extract oil. Seed extract oil is derived from neem seeds and is known for its potent insecticidal and antifungal properties, making it popular in agriculture and skincare. Leaf extract oil is obtained from neem leaves, valued for its medicinal benefits and use in natural remedies. Bark extract oil, though less common, is extracted from the bark and is utilized for its antibacterial and anti-inflammatory effects. Each type serves unique applications across various industries.</p></p>
