<p><strong>The Doppler LiDAR Systems Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Pulse</li><li>Continuous Wave</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Doppler LiDAR systems utilize different techniques for measuring velocity and distance. The pulse-type emits short bursts of laser light, capturing reflections to determine speed and distance, making it suitable for long-range applications. In contrast, continuous wave LiDAR continuously emits laser signals, enabling real-time velocity measurements through frequency shifts in the returned light, offering higher accuracy for shorter ranges. Both types serve diverse industries, including autonomous vehicles, environmental monitoring, and atmospheric research, each catering to specific measurement needs and operational scenarios.</p></p>
