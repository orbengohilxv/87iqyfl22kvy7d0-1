<p><strong>The Air Starter Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Turbine Starter</li><li>Vane Starter</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The air starter market is primarily categorized into two types: turbine starters and vane starters. Turbine starters utilize high-velocity air to turn a turbine, providing reliable and efficient engine starting, especially in heavy-duty applications like gas turbines. Vane starters, on the other hand, use a series of vanes that expand with pressurized air to drive the output shaft. Both types offer advantages such as reduced risk of electrical hazards and improved performance in challenging environments, making them essential in various industrial applications.</p></p>
