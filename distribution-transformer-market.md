<p><strong>Market Segmentation</strong></p>
<p><strong>The Distribution Transformer Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Dry</li><li>Liquid immersed</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The distribution transformer market is primarily segmented into two types: dry type and liquid-immersed transformers. Dry type transformers utilize air for cooling, making them ideal for indoor applications and environments with fire safety concerns. These transformers are typically maintenance-free and environmentally friendly. In contrast, liquid-immersed transformers are filled with insulating oil, providing effective heat dissipation and greater capacity for larger power loads. They are commonly used in outdoor settings and are suitable for high voltage applications, where durability and reliability are essential.</p></p>
